Our Story

& Vision

Transforming discarded fabrics into wearable art

Our journey began with a simple vision: to give fabric a second life. We source discarded textiles and transform them into wearable art—statement jackets, handcrafted jewelry, unique bags, and home décor pieces that celebrate individuality. Each Bonis Rubatosis creation is meticulously handmade, ensuring no two pieces are exactly alike. This is luxury redefined—not through mass production, but through careful craftsmanship and artistic vision. From our Turkey Tail jewelry sets to our signature Maxi Jackets and upcycled accessories, every item embodies our commitment to sustainability and beauty. We work with passion, love, and respect for both people and nature. When you wear Bonis Rubatosis, you are not just wearing fashion—you are wearing a piece of art, a story of transformation, and a commitment to a more sustainable future.
